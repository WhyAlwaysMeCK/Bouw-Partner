About me

I started with complete construction and finishing work and now focus mainly on timberwork in the Netherlands.

In Poland I worked on own projects and broad finishing work. In the Netherlands the direction shifted more and more into specialist work: timberwork, prefab installation, frames, sloped roofs and facade finishing.

Experience in Poland

First own projects and complete finishing work.

The first years were broad practical work: interiors, demolition, drywall, ceilings, painting, floors, windows, doors and installation work.

  • interiors and room arrangement
  • drywall and ceilings
  • floors, painting and wallpaper
  • electrical, hydraulic and shower-related work

Experience in the Netherlands

Then more than 5 years of timberwork in the Netherlands.

In the Netherlands the work became more specialist: timber structures, modular parts, prefab wall and roof elements, frames, sloped roofs and front facade finishing.

  • timberwork and prefab installation
  • frames, windows and doors
  • timber-look, HPL, Rockpanel and composite
  • sloped roofs and visible details

Today

Today I mainly take on work where dimensions, installation and finishing stay visible.

That is where the experience from both Poland and the Netherlands is most useful: prefab, frames, facade cladding and sloped roofs.
